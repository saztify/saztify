I really enjoy fishing as my hobby. It's a peaceful escape from the busyness of life. I get to be in nature, listening to the water and birds. When I cast my line, it's like a little adventure every time. I have to be patient, waiting for a fish to bite. Fishing helps me connect with nature and appreciate the world around me. It's not just about catching fish, but also finding calm and happiness by the water. It's a simple joy in my life, and it's something I love doing.

Since 2015, playing basketball has been one of my favourite pastimes, and I do it practically every night. Over time, it has developed into a genuine passion from what started out as a simple infatuation. The court has grown to be my second home, a heaven where I can go to escape the rigours of everyday life with its well-known lines and hoops.

Since 2016, when I received my very first phone capable of supporting mobile applications, I've immersed myself in the world of mobile gaming.
